# Product Guidelines: MetaChat

## Messaging and Tone
- **Professional & Academic:** All communications, documentation, and interface text should be clear, precise, and authoritative. Use terminology consistent with nanophotonics and computational electromagnetics research.
- **Accurate and Precise:** Avoid hyperbole. Focus on the empirical benefits and the technical capabilities of the AIM and FiLM WaveY-Net systems.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity over Decoration:** The user interface must prioritize the effective communication of complex scientific data. Use clear information hierarchies, high-quality data visualizations, and minimize unnecessary decorative elements.
- **Functional Aesthetic:** Design elements should serve a purpose in helping the user understand the design space, the simulation results, and the agentic process.

## Core Values
- **Scientific Rigor:** The product must always communicate its commitment to physical accuracy. Ensure that results from surrogate solvers are presented alongside appropriate context regarding their fidelity to Maxwell's equations.
- **Accessibility:** While maintaining rigor, the system should strive to make complex freeform design tasks more approachable. This is achieved through the intuitive agentic monologue and the rapid feedback loop.
- **Efficiency:** The core value proposition is the dramatic reduction in design time. Every aspect of the user experience should reinforce the speed and fluidity of the MetaChat workflow.
